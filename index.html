MemoryFlip

Flip cards, remember their positions, and match every pair in as few moves as possible.

How to Play

  1. Press New Game to shuffle the board.
  2. Click any two cards to reveal their symbols.
  3. If the symbols match, the cards stay visible.
  4. If they do not match, they flip back after a short delay.
  5. Match all 8 pairs to win the game.
